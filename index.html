About the Program

The AiNTERN Portal connects learners, recruiters, and institutions through an AI-powered allocation engine that matches profiles to opportunities based on skills, interests, and goals. Our approach emphasizes transparency and equal access, using explainable models and anonymized matching to reduce bias. Students discover relevant roles faster, recruiters reach motivated talent at scale, and institutions gain insights that help improve curricula and outcomes.
